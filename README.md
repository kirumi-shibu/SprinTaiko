# ü•Å SprinTaiko

A web-based rhythm game inspired by "Taiko no Tatsujin" and typing practice software.
The goal is to hit the notes that flow from the right as quickly and accurately as possible.
It's designed to be a fun way to practice and measure your typing speed and rhythm skills.

## üéÆ How to Play

1. Press the **Start** button or the **Enter** key to begin the game.
2. When the notes reach the target circle on the left, press the corresponding keys.
    - <kbd>F</kbd> or <kbd>J</kbd> for **Don** (red notes)
    - <kbd>D</kbd> or <kbd>K</kbd> for **Ka** (blue notes)
3. The game ends when all notes have been processed. Your score will be calculated based on your speed (KPS - Keys Per Second) and accuracy.
4. You can press the **Esc** key at any time to interrupt and reset the game.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Adjustable Hi-Speed:** Change the speed at which notes travel.
- **Customizable Note Count:** Set the number of notes for each game session (from 10 to 999).
- **Scoring and Ranking:** Your score is based on speed and accuracy. The top 5 scores are saved locally in your browser.
- **Sound Customization:**
  - Adjust the master volume.
  - Upload your own sound files for "Don", "Ka", "Miss", and other game sounds.
- **Persistent Settings:** Your Hi-Speed, note count, and sound settings are saved in your browser for the next time you play.
